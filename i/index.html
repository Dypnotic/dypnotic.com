What's a Dypnotic?

Two years later, the French duo released the remix album Daft Club. It features an unplugged version of the hit track, with lyrics that were slightly altered, perhaps even improvised, especially in the last verse:

Why Japanese?

なぜ日本語なのか

At the time of writing, I am currently learning Japanese. As a huge gaming and anime enthusiast I want to be able to watch and understand Japanese media without having a layer of translation. Using Japanese in titles and occasionally elsewhere is my way of having fun with it, so I do not get bored.
